ABOUT

Holly Carter

I spend most of my days creating art and design work, as well as, learning to craft my new passion for UX. I am currently in the process of earning a Masters in Visual Communication at ASU. I grew up in a rural area in West Virginia where most of my days were spent daydreaming. I’m constantly trying to learn new methods to create visually stimulating work to enhance my design and art skills. I believe in balancing traditional with innovative design methods because without both I wouldn’t be able to learn where the industry has been and were it will take me in the future.
