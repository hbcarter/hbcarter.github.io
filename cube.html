CUBE PROJECT

Presenting the three-dimensional layout, I had to create a dynamic desigin using multiples that would interact with the viewers perception of depth. Using the manipulation of scale and wieght to give the already three-dimensional cubes another element of depth. Each side of the cube was symmetrical designed to not only move from side to sided but also move in and out of each side of the cube.The cube project process showed the many dynamics of designing a three-dimensional object on a two-dimensional plane. The image makeing process of making a phsyical cube showed us elements of the process of package design and the thought process that makes a well designed pakage.
